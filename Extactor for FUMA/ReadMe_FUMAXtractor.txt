The FUMAXtractor works specifically for the dataset by Kristiansen et al (2024).

Link to the dataset: https://zenodo.org/records/10794443

The CSV files included within the folder are outputs based on the IPYNB script and the FutureMares
dataset as obtained from the above link.

Ensure that you use your own filepath in syncronisation with the script written.

For more information on the dataset, please see their publication:
Statistically downscaled CMIP6 ocean variables for European waters
https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-024-51160-1
